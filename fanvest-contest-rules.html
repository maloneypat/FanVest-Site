Summary

  • Free to play. Educational/entertainment only; no prizes or monetary value.
  • Open to all users. No age or location restrictions; void where prohibited.
  • Public leaderboards. Recognition is informational only.

Eligibility

  • Open to all users; by participating you agree to these Rules and the FanVest Terms of Use; void where prohibited.

How It Works

  • Enter in the FanVest app during the posted entry window; limit one (1) entry per Contest.
  • Construct a portfolio by assigning weights (totaling 100%) across the eligible securities.
  • Entries lock at the Contest start; results use publicly available market data and uniform rounding.

Scoring & Leaderboards

  • Scores are based on weighted closing-price performance during the Contest period.
  • Leaderboards are informational only and confer no prize, status, or value.
  • If final returns are identical, entrants share the same rank.

Publicity & Social Media

  • FanVest may share aggregate rankings and top usernames on social media and FanVest-owned channels.
  • We do not disclose personal contact information or data beyond what appears in the in-app leaderboard.
