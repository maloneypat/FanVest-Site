FanVest Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 29, 2025

FanVest (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the FanVest mobile application (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Contest Participation Data: We store your contest entries, portfolio allocations, and leaderboard results to operate contests.
  • Device & Usage Data: Basic diagnostic and crash data may be collected automatically by Apple to help us improve performance.
  • iCloud / CloudKit Data: Your contest data and profile information may be synced securely using Apple’s CloudKit services.

We do not collect payment information or sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Information

  • Run free fantasy investment contests and leaderboards.
  • Sync your contest data across your devices via iCloud.
  • Improve and support the App.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

  • With Apple Services: We rely on Apple services (iCloud/CloudKit, TestFlight) for secure storage and testing.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

We do not share user information with advertisers or data brokers.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Contest data is stored in Apple’s CloudKit public and private databases, protected by Apple’s security infrastructure.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

5. Your Choices

  • If you wish to delete your FanVest data, please contact us at info@fanvestapp.com.
  • You can manage iCloud settings on your device in Settings > [your name] > iCloud.

6. Children’s Privacy

FanVest is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
