1. Sponsor & Administrator

  • The FanVest Fantasy Contest ("Contest") is sponsored and administered by FanVest, LLC ("Sponsor"). Questions or comments regarding the Contest should be directed to Sponsor via info@fanvestapp.com.

2. Nature of the Contest (No Prizes; Educational/Entertainment Only)

  • The Contest is free to play and offered solely for educational and entertainment purposes.
  • No prizes, awards, or monetary value (collectively, “Prizes”) are offered or distributed.
  • The Contest involves simulated portfolio selections and public leaderboards only. Any recognition is purely informational and confers no tangible value.
  • The Contest does not involve real-money wagering, sweepstakes, or gambling.

3. Eligibility

  • Participation is open to all users, with no age or residency/location restrictions.
  • By participating, you represent that your participation complies with any laws or rules applicable to you and with the FanVest Terms of Use.
  • The Contest is void where prohibited by law.
  • Sponsor may restrict or terminate access to maintain Contest integrity or to comply with applicable laws.

4. Contest Period

  • Each Contest period begins and ends on the dates posted in the FanVest app.
  • Sponsor will post the official start and end dates for each Contest within the FanVest app.

5. How to Enter (Free to Play)

  • NO PURCHASE NECESSARY. Download or open the FanVest app, navigate to the Fantasy Invest section, and complete the Contest entry for the active Contest.
  • FanVest offers two Contest formats: Top 5 Stocks and Fantasy 500 Pick Six. The format for each Contest is displayed in the app.
  • Limit one (1) entry per person per Contest. Entries that are incomplete, inaccurate, late, or generated by automated means will be void.

6. Portfolio Construction

  • Top 5 Stocks format: Entrants must assign a percentage weighting to each of the five (5) eligible securities listed for the Contest. The total of all weightings must equal exactly 100%.
  • Fantasy 500 Pick Six format: Entrants must select six (6) stocks from the S&P 500. Each selection is weighted equally by the app.
  • Entries may be edited within the app until 11:59 p.m. U.S. Eastern Time on the day before the Contest start date. Once the Contest begins, entries are locked for the duration of the Contest Period.

7. Scoring & Leaderboards (Informational Only)

  • Each entrant’s portfolio return is calculated using the daily closing price performance of the selected securities and the weightings for the applicable Contest format.
  • For Top 5 Stocks, the weightings are the percentages assigned by the entrant.
  • For Fantasy 500 Pick Six, the app applies equal weighting across the six selected stocks.
  • Sponsor will calculate weighted returns using publicly available market data and may apply uniform rounding conventions.
  • Leaderboards show relative performance for informational purposes only.
  • References to a “top-ranked” entrant reflect leaderboard standing only and do not confer any prize, award, or status outside the app.
  • In the event of identical final returns, entrants will share the same rank on the leaderboard (no tie‑breakers or drawings are conducted).

8. Notifications (No Winner Verification Required)

  • Sponsor may send in‑app notifications about leaderboard positions or Contest summaries.
  • Because no prizes are offered, there is no winner verification or affidavit process.

9. No Prizes or Consideration

  • There are no prizes of any kind. No substitution, assignment, or transfer applies.
  • Participants are not responsible for taxes related to Contest participation because no prizes are awarded.

  • Sponsor may cancel, suspend, or modify the Contest if fraud, technical failures, human error, market data issues, or any other factor impairs the integrity or proper functioning of the Contest.
  • Sponsor may disqualify any individual who tampers with the entry process, attempts to circumvent limitations, uses automated tools, or otherwise violates these Rules or the Terms of Use.

11. Privacy
